RGSS Computer Studies

Find out about CS @ RGSS!

Are you interested in taking computer studies at Richmond Green?
Click on the link to play the game, and speak to some virtual teachers who can give a bit more information about the courses offered at Richmond Green Secondary School.

On your computer:
Use the WASD keys to move yourself around the gym.
Talk to people and pick up items using the Spacebar.

On your phone or tablet:
Move by tapping on the screen. Pick up items by tapping on them.
People will talk to you when you wait for them to speak. Go to the virtual gym
